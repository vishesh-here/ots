Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a founding member

Founding members help shape our mission, provide strategic input, participate in key decisions, and contribute their skills and networks to build the organization. The time commitment is flexible and based on your availability and interests.

While financial contributions are welcome and helpful, they are not mandatory to become a founding member. We value diverse forms of contribution including time, expertise, networks, and passion for our mission.

We plan to establish regional chapters and partnerships with local organizations to ensure our programs are culturally relevant and effectively implemented. Our approach emphasizes local leadership while maintaining consistent quality and values.

Our focus on empowering individuals to "own their story" goes beyond traditional education. We emphasize self-reliance, cultural pride, and long-term transformation rather than dependency. We're also religion-agnostic and committed to serving all communities equally.

We're currently in the founding phase, building our team and establishing partnerships. Pilot programs are expected to begin within the next 6-12 months, with founding members having input on program design and implementation locations.
