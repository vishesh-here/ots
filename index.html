Our Mission

With this belief at our core, we are on a mission to empower underprivileged youth from across India. Own The Story is an all-India, religion-agnostic society committed to transforming the broken educational infrastructure that serves the majority with a minority of resources.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our mission

Equity

Ensuring fair access to quality education for all, regardless of background or circumstance.

Empathy

Understanding and addressing the unique challenges faced by underprivileged communities.

Self-reliance

Empowering individuals to become independent and confident in their abilities.

Cultural Pride

Celebrating and preserving the rich diversity of Indian culture and heritage.

Our Story

The journey that led to Own The Story

The Recognition

We recognized that millions of talented young minds across India were being held back not by lack of potential, but by systemic barriers in educational access and quality. The majority were being served by a minority of resources.

Identifying the gap between potential and opportunity

The Vision

We envisioned a movement that would go beyond traditional charity models. Instead of temporary fixes, we aimed to create lasting transformation through education, empowerment, and belief in every individual's ability to own their story.

Envisioning sustainable transformation

The Foundation

Own The Story was born as an all-India, religion-agnostic society. We established our core values of equity, empathy, self-reliance, and cultural pride as the foundation for everything we do.

Building on strong values and principles

The Movement

Today, we're building a movement of TaleSmiths who believe in the power of education to transform lives. Together, we're writing a story worth owning - one that future generations will be proud to inherit.

Creating a community of changemakers

What is a TaleSmith?

The architects of transformation

Crafting Stories of Change

A TaleSmith is more than a founding member – they are the master craftspeople who forge narratives of hope, transformation, and empowerment. Just as a blacksmith shapes metal into tools of purpose, TaleSmiths shape stories into instruments of change.

Every TaleSmith understands that behind every statistic is a story waiting to be rewritten. They believe that when we change the story, we change the outcome. When we own our narrative, we own our destiny.
